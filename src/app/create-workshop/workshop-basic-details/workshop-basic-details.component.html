Describe your experience

Your activity description is a chance to inspire learners to take your experience. Think about it like a story, with a biginning, middle and end.

1. First, briefly describe what you'll do with your learners. What unique details set it apart from other similar experiences?

2. Then, get more specific. How will you kick things off? How will you make guests feel engaged during your time together?

3. Finally, explain what you want your learners will leave with and how they can take their newfound knowledge further. End with a strong selling point.
