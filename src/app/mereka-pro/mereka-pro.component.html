If you want to create an hub and sell the services of other workers, you’ll first need to create an Expert account for the hub owner.

Once that account activated:

1. Go to Settings > My Teams

2. Select the Create a new account button at the bottom

3. Select the New Expert Hub Account option and fill out the form

After you create your Hub, the account menu will be where you can switch between your personal and hub account. A person can only have one Hub Mereka. You won’t be able to make another one.
